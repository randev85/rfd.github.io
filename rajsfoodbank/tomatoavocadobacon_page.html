Bacon, Avocado & Tomato Salad

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Smoked Bacon 4 Rashes
Ripe Tomatoes 400g
Baby Spinach Leaves 100g
Avocado 2x1
Lemon 1x1
Olive Oil 40mlg
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 4-6 portions

Preparation

  • Wash and spin dry baby spinach leaves.
  • Cut tomato into 1/8's.
  • Juice lemon.
  • Deseed, peel and cut avocado into 1cm cubes. Coat in lemon juice.
  • Coat tomato 1/8's in 20ml of oil and season with salt and pepper before placing on a baking tray lined with grease proof paper.
  • Place rashes of bacon on a baking tray lined with grease proof paper.
  • Pre-heat oven to 170â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place tomato in the oven for 15-18 minutes until golden brown.
  • Place tray of bacon under the grill and cook for 3 minutes each side until both side are golden brown. Allow to cool to room temperature before finely shredding with a sharp knife.
  • Allow tomato to cool to room temperature.
  • Mix tomato, spinach, avocado, bacon and remaining olive oil before seasoning to taste with salt and pepper.

Serving Suggestions

Serve alongside a leafy salad.

Storing instructions

Store in an airtight contain before placing in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
