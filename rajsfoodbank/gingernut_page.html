Gingernut Biscuits

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Self-raising Flour 220g
Ginger Powder 2 tsp
Bicarbonate of Soda 2 level tsp
Caster Sugar 80g
Unsalted Butter 100g
Table salt ¼ tsp
Golden Syrup 100g

Yield: 32 biscuits

Preparation

  • Place butter in the microwave for 20-30 seconds until soft.
  • In a bowl, sift flour, ground ginger, bicarbonate of soda and add sugar before working in butter with finger tips.
  • You will be left with a bread crumb consistency and it is now that you add the golden syrup and mix thoroughly.
  • Line baking tray with greaseproof paper.
  • Use a dessert spoon to portion out mixture on to baking tray. Remember to leave an inch perimeter around each blob.
  • Pre-heat oven to 180â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place baking tray in the centre of oven and cook for 10-15 minutes.
  • Remove from oven carefully transfer to wire rack to cool for 30 minutes.

Serving Suggestions

Gingernut biscuits goes great with an English cup of tea.

Storing instructions

Store in an airtight container and place in dry place. Consume within 2 weeks of making it.
