Croque Monsieur

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Thick Sliced Bread 2x1
Slice Gammon 2 slices
Gruyere Cheese 60g
Unsalted Butter 20g
Dijon Mustard 10g

Yield: 1 portions

Preparation

  • Grate Gruyere cheese.
  • Place unsalted butter in microwave for 10 seconds to soften.
  • Place 1st slice of bread down and layer sliced gammon on top of it. Sprinkle grate gruyere cheese over ham and before placing the 2nd bread on top, apply mustard to the underside of the bread.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a medium sized pan over a medium/high heat.
  • Butter topside of sandwich and place top side down in the pan. Reduce heat to low/medium and butter the remaining side of sandwich.
  • Fry first side for 4-5 minutes before flipping over. Make sure both sides are golden brown and gruyere cheese has melted before serving

Serving Suggestions

Croque Monsieur goes great with a cup of soup.

Storing instructions

Consume when made.
