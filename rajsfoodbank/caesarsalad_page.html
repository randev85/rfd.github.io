Chicken Caesar Salad

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breast 2 fillet
Bacon 2 Rashes
Sliced White Bread 3 Slices
Parmesan Cheese 80g
Baby Gem Lettuce 2 heads
Caesar Dressing 70g
Vegetable Oil 50ml
Garlic Powder 2 tsp
Salt & Pepper To Season

Yield: 2 portions

Preparation

  • Tear, wash and spin dry baby gem leaves.
  • Coat chicken breast with 20ml vegetable oil and season with salt and pepper.
  • Place rashes of bacon on baking tray with lined grease proof paper.
  • Cut sliced bread into 1cm cubes, coast with remaining vegetable oil, all the garlic powder and season with salt and pepper. Evenly distribute in a baking tray lined with grease proof paper.
  • Shave Parmesan cheese.
  • Pre-heat oven to 160’C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place baking tray with cubed bread in the oven for 15 minutes. Give crotons a shake after 8 minutes for an even drying.
  • Turn up heat of oven to 180’C.
  • Place an ovenproof skillet on the stove over a medium/high heat. Once skillet is hot, fry chicken breasts for 2 minutes per side ensuring they’re golden brown . Place skillet in the oven for 6-8 minutes. Allow to cool before slicing to a 5mm thickness
  • Place bacon under grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side until crispy golden brown. Finely shred with sharp knife.
  • Mix baby gem leaves, half the bacon, half the chicken, half the crotons and half the shaved parmesan cheese with the Caesar dressing. Add to serving dish and top with the remaining chicken, bacon, crotons, and parmesan cheese.

Serving Suggestions

Serve on it’s own.

Storing instructions

Consume when made.
