Carrot Cake

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable Oil 170ml
Self-raining Flour 230g
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Ground Cinnamon 2 tsp
Mixed Spice ½ tsp
Ginger Powder ½ tsp
Soft Light Brown Sugar 230g
Orange 1x1
Walnuts 100g
Carrots 260g
Medium Eggs 3x1
Butter 60g
Mascarpone Cheese 200g
Icing Sugar 100g

Yield: 8-10 portions

Preparation

  • Zest and juice orange.
  • Peel and grate carrots.
  • Break walnuts into 6-8 pieces each.
  • Sift flour, cinnamon powder, mixed spice, ground ginger and baking powder into bowl.
  • Line a 18cm spring form baking tin with grease proof paper.
  • Add sugar, orange zest, walnuts and grated carrots to bowl containing flour.
  • Beat in eggs and 160ml of vegetable oil before combining with dry ingerdients well.
  • Pre-heat oven to 160â€™C.
  • Add batter to cake tin.
  • Warm butter in microwave for 10 seconds.
  • Use an electric whisk to mix butter, mascarpone, icing sugar and orange juice. Start on a slow speed and once icing sugar is combined, turn up speed and whisk until pale and fluffy.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place cake tins in the oven for 55-60 minutes. Use a skewer to check if the centre is cooked
  • Allow the cake to cool and remove from cake tins.
  • Once carrot cake has cooled, cut in half horizontally and spread half of the mascarpone mixture. Place the second half of the carrot cake on top of the mascarpone and finish by applying the remaining amount of cream cheese to the top of the whole cake.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with a cup of tea.

Storing instructions

Cover with clingfilm and place in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
