Tuna Mayonnaise

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Tuna Chunks in Spring Water 2x160g
Mayonnaise 60g
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 380g

Preparation

  • Remove and drain tuna from tins.
  • Mix tuna with mayonnaise and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Serving Suggestions

Use as a filling for sandwiches or baguettes.

Storing instructions

Store in an airtight contain before placing in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
