Tiramisu

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Americano coffee 200ml
Amaretto Liquor 30ml
Double Cream 450ml
Mascarpone Cheese 300g
Caster Sugar 50g
Baileys Irish Cream 60ml
Vanilla Essence 1 tsp
Sponge Fingers 180g
Cocoa Powder 40g

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Allow americano coffee to cool to room temperature and add amaretto liquor to it.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk mascarpone cheese, double cream, sugar and baileys until a soft peak is achieved. Transfer to a piping bag with a 1cm diameter nozzle on it.
  • Dip sponge finger into americano for a few second on each side before apply to the bottom of a serving dish. Repeat process until first layer is complete.
  • Pipe cream mixture as the second layer to about 1 inch in depth.
  • Repeat the process for soaking the sponge fingers and placing a third layer to the tiramisu .
  • Use the remaining cream to pipe peaks onto the second layer of sponge finger. Ensure all peaks are similar in height. Fish the tiramisu by dusting with cocoa powder and placing in the fridge for 1 hour.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with a cup of coffee.

Storing instructions

Cover with clingfilm and place in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
