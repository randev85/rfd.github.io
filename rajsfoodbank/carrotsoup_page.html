Carrot Soup

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable Oil 40ml
White Onion 1x1
Celery 2 stalks
Garlic Cloves 3 Cloves
Carrots 1.2kg
Vegetable Stock 1.4 litres
Dried Thyme 1 tsp
Ground Coriander 2 tsp
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and slice onion.
  • Wash and slice celery stalks.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Peel and slices carrots into equal sized pieces.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a large saucepan over a medium/high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Add onion, celery and carrots before stirring and cooking for 5 minutes.
  • Once vegetables have softened, add thyme, coriander and crush garlic before cooking for a further 3 minutes.
  • Vegetables should smell aromatic and at this stage, add vegetable stock to submerge vegetables. Add water if needed.
  • Place a lid over the saucepan and bring to the boil before reducing heat to a simmer. Cook for 30-35 minutes.
  • Once carrots are soft to the touch, place in a food processor or alternatively, use a stick blender and blend smooth. Add water if soup is too thick.
  • Once desired consistency is achieved, pass soup through a chinois to strain out any big bits.
  • Return to the pan and season to taste.

Serving Suggestions

Carrot soup goes great with crusty bread and plenty of butter.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature before placing in an airtight container and placing in the fridge. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Gently bring back up to temperature in saucepan on stove. Alternatively, warm in microwave for 3 minutes, stirring every 1 minute to ensure even warming.
