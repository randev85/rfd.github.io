Sweet Potato & Chickpea Curry

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable Oil 40ml
Cumin Seeds 30g
White Onion 2x1
Garlic Cloves 4 Cloves
Dried Chilli Flakes 2 tsp
Root Ginger 2 Inches
Tinned Chickpeas 2x400g
Tinned Chopped Tomatoes 2x400g
Sweet Potato 800g
Vegetable Stock 500ml
Coriander 30g
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and finely slice white onions.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Peel and grate root ginger.
  • Drain and wash tinned chickpeas.
  • Peel and cut sweet potato into 1 inch cubes.
  • Pick coriander leaves.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a saucepan over a medium/high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Add cumin seeds and fry for 1-2 minutes until golden brown and aromatic.
  • Add sliced onion and a ½ a teaspoon of salt before cooking for 4-5 minutes until onions are translucent.
  • Add garlic, ginger and chilli flakes before cooking for a further 5 minutes.
  • Add chickpeas, tinned tomatoes, sweet potato and vegetable stock to the pan. Bring to the boil and reduce to the simmer with lid on for 25 minutes.
  • Season to taste with salt and pepper before garnishing with coriander.

Serving Suggestions

This dish goes great with naan bread, roti or pilaf rice.

Storing instructions

Place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave for 2-3 minutes.
