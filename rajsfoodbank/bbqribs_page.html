BBQ Ribs

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Pork Ribs 1.5kg
Paprika 10g
Garlic Powder 20g
Cayenne Pepper 1 tsp
Table Salt 1 tsp
Vegetable Oil 40ml
BBQ Sauce 400ml

Yield: 5-6 portions

Preparation

  • Mix paprika, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, salt and vegetable oil.
  • Rub Ribs with paprika oil and place on an elevated wire rack before placing the wire rack in a roasting tin.
  • Add 500ml of water to roasting tin and cover with 2 layers of foil, ensuring a tight seal.
  • Pre-heat oven to 160’C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place roasting tin in oven for 3 hours.
  • Remove foil from tin and brush on generous amount of BBQ sauce.
  • Turn up heat of oven to 200’C and bake ribs for 20 minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve ribs with green beans and chips.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature, seal in an airtight container and place in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making.

Reheating Instructions

Heat in microwave for 2-3 minutes.
