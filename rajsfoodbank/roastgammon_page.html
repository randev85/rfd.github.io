Roasted Gammon

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Gammon Joint 2¾kg
Dijon Mustard 25g
Honey 40g

Yield: 8-10 portions

Preparation

  • Place gammon joint in cold water and in the fridge for 24 hours to extract some of it’s salt.
  • Drain gammon of any excess water and place in a roasting tin. Apply 2 layers of foil to the top of the tin ensuring it is loose but tightly wrapped around the edges.
  • Pre-heat oven to 160’C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place roasting tin in the oven for 2 hours.
  • Remove joint from oven and increase the temperature to 200’C.
  • Transfer join to chopping board and cut the outer layer skin off being careful not to take anything away from the fat layer.
  • Score slashes into the fat layer of the gammon and mix the mustard and honey before applying to surface.
  • Remove any liquid from roasting tin and return gammon joint to it. Place tin in the oven for 25 minutes until gammon looks brown and glazed.
  • Remove from the oven and allow to rest for 20 minutes under foil before carving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve as main component of Sunday lunch.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature before placing in an airtight container and placing in the fridge. Consume within 7 days of making it.
