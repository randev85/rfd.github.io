Red Cabbage Slaw

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Red Cabbage 1x1
Spring Onions 3 Stalks
Carrots 2x1
Olive Oil 80ml
Dijon Mustard 15g
Cumin Seeds 15g
Cider Vinegar 10ml
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Core and finely shred red cabbage.
  • Finely slice spring onion on the diagonal.
  • Peel and grate carrots.
  • Mix carrot, cabbage and spring onion with mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, cumin seeds and cider vinegar before seasoning with salt and pepper.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with a burger and chips.

Storing instructions

Store in an airtight contain before placing in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
