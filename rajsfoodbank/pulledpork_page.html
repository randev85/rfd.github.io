Pulled Pork

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Boneless Pork shoulder 2½kg
Smoked Paprika 2 tsp
Ground Cumin 2 tsp
Cracked Black Pepper 2 tsp
Dark Brown Sugar 2 tsp
Table Salt 1 tsp
Apple Cider 500ml

Yield: 12-14 portions

Preparation

  • Mix smoked paprika, ground cumin, black pepper, brown sugar and salt together.
  • Cut pork shoulder into 6 equal pieces, rub with oil and paprika mix.
  • Pre-heat oven to 160â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a large frying pan over a medium/high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Brown off pork shoulder pieces and place on elevated wire wrack before placing wire rack in deep roasting tin.
  • Pour apple cider into bottom of roasting tin and cover tin with 2 layers of foil. Ensure foils is tightly wrapper around edges
  • Place roasting tin in oven and cook for 4-5 hours.
  • Remove from oven, dispose of foil and remove skin from shoulder pieces.
  • Pulled pork apart, add some of the juices from the roasting pan and season to taste.

Serving Suggestions

Serve in a bap with BBQ sauce.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature before placing in an airtight container and placing in the fridge. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Warm in microwave for 3 minutes.
