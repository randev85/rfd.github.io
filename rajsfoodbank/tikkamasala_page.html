Chicken Tikka Masala

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable Oil 45ml
Chicken Breast 6 fillets
White Onion 3x1
Tikka Paste 235g
Natural Yoghurt 200g
Green Chillies 2x1
Garlic Cloves 3 Cloves
Root Ginger 2 Inches
Tomato Puree 45g
Lemon ½x1
Coriander 50g
Tinned Chopped Tomatoes 2x400g
Garam Masala ½ tsp
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Cut chicken breast into 1 inch cubes.
  • Marinade chicken in the yoghurt and half the tikka paste for at least 2 hours, preferably 24 hours.
  • Peel and finely dice white onion.
  • Finely slice green chillies.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Peel and grate and root ginger.
  • Pick coriander leaves.
  • Juice half a Lemon.
  • Place marinated chicken on a baking tray.
  • Pre-heat oven to 190â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place tray of chicken in the oven for 15 minutes.
  • Put a medium sized saucepan on a medium/high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Add onions, chillies, garlic and ginger before frying for 5 minutes.
  • Add the remaining tikka paste and the tomato puree and cook out for 2 minutes.
  • Add tin tomatoes, bring to the boil and reduce to the simmer for 20 minutes.
  • Add chicken to the thicken tomato sauce and simmer away for a further 10 minutes.
  • Sprinkle over garam masala, pour over lemon juice and add coriander leaves. Leave for 2 minutes before tasting and seasoning.

Serving Suggestions

Chicken Tikka can be served with naan, roti or rice.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave and heat for 3-4 minutes.
