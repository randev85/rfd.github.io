Smoked Mackerel Paté

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Smoked Mackrel Fillets 4x1
Dijon Mustard 20g
Créme Fraiche 60g
Unsalted Butter 200g
Lemon 1x1

Yield: 320g

Preparation

  • Remove skin from mackerel.
  • Juice lemon.
  • Soften butter in microwave for 30 seconds.
  • Place mackerel, mustard, lemon juice, créme fraiche and butter in a food processor and blend smooth.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with crusty bread and butter

Storing instructions

Place in an air-tight container and in a refrigerator. Consume within 1 week of making.
