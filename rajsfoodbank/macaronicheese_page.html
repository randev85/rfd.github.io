Macaroni Cheese

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Macaroni 250g
Béchamel Sauce 600g
Dijon Mustard 2 tsp
Mature Cheddar Cheese 300g
Salt & Pepper To Season

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Grate mature cheddar cheese.
  • Pre-heat oven to 170â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place medium sized pan over a medium/high heat. Add hot water, bring to the boil and season with salt. Add macaroni and cook for 6 minutes until al dente.
  • In a separate saucepan, gently bring béchamel sauce up to heat. Drain macaroni and add it to béchamel sauce before adding 200g of grated mature cheddar cheese and Dijon mustard. Stir until cheese has melted before seasoning to taste.
  • Add mixture to deep baking dish and top with remaining grated mature cheddar cheese.
  • Place in the oven 20-25 minutes until cheese is golden brown.

Serving Suggestions

Goes great with garlic bread.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature before placing in an air-tight container and in the fridge. Consume rice within 4 days of making.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave for 2-3 minutes.
