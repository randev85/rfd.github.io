Beef Burritos

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Beef Chilli 600g
Sour Cream & Chives 80g
Tomato Salsa 80g
Red Pepper 1x1
Green Pepper 1x1
Yellow Pepper 1x1
Red Onion 1x1
12‚Äù Tortilla Wraps 4x1
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 4 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and finely slice red onion.
  • Deseed and slice peppers.

Cooking Instructions

  • Put a medium sized saucepan on a medium/high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Add red onions and peppers before frying for 2 minutes, seasoning to taste and removing from heat.
  • Place beef chilli in saucepan on a gentle heat and warm through thoroughly.
  • Place tortilla wraps under grill for 20 seconds each side.
  • Compile Burrito by starting with a tortilla wrap at the bottom, adding some sautéed pepper, adding some sour cream, spooning on some salsa and adding some Beef chilli. Finish by firstly, wrapping up the tortilla, and secondly, wrapping that within a layer of foil.

Serving Suggestions

I love serving my burritos with either sweet potato wedges or piri-piri fries.

Storing instructions

Consume on the day.
