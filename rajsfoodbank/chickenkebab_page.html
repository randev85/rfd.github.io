Chicken Kebabs

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breast 6 fillets
Unsalted Butter 200g
Garlic Powder 20g
Cayenne Pepper 1 tsp
Smoked Paprika 20g
Salt & Pepper To Taste
Button Mushrooms 12 Mushrooms
Red Onion 3x1
Courgettes 1x1
Aubergine 1x1
Red Pepper 1x1
Green Pepper 1x1
Yellow Pepper 1x1

Yield: 10-12 portions

Preparation

  • Cut chicken breast into 6 equal cubes.
  • Cut red onions into 8 equal picecs.
  • Cut courgettes into 24 equal pieces.
  • Cut aubergine into 24 equal pieces.
  • Deseed and cut red pepper into 12 equal pieces.
  • Deseed and cut green pepper into 12 equal pieces.
  • Deseed and cut yellow peppers into 12 equal pieces.
  • Place 3 portions of cubed chicken, 2 portions of red onion, 2 portions of courgette, 2 portions of aubergine and 1 of each portion of pepper on a wooden skewer. Repeat until 12 skewers are made.
  • Melt butter in microwave and add garlic powder, cayenne pepper smoked paprika before seasoning butter to taste.
  • Brush flavoured butter on skewers and place on baking trays.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place chicken skewers on baking tray and under the grill. Cook for 5 minutes per side.

Serving Suggestions

Chicken kebabs can be served with cous cous, pitta bread or cumin rice.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave and heat foe 2-3 minutes.
