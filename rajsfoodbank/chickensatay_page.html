Chicken Satay

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breast 6 fillets
Peanut Butter 40g
Dark Soy Sauce 100ml
Lemon 3x1
Soft Dark Sugar 15g
Mild Curry Powder 30g
Garlic Cloves 2 Cloves
Chilli Sauce 1 tsp
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 10-12 portions

Preparation

  • Cut chicken into 4 strips.
  • Juice lemons.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Mix peanut butter, soy sauce, lemon juice, brown sugar, curry powder, crushed garlic and chilli sauce together. Season to taste.
  • Mix chicken with satay paste and leave to marinade for 2 hours in refrigerator.
  • Needle 10 inch skewers through 2 strips of chicken satay and repeat this until 12 skewers are prepared.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place chicken skewers on baking tray and under the grill. Cook for 5 minutes per side.

Serving Suggestions

Chicken Satay can be served with stir-fried vegetables or with sticky rice.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave and heat foe 2-3 minutes.
