Strawberry Milkshake

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Whole Milk 200ml
Strawberries 100g
Vanilla Ice Cream 150g

Yield: 500ml

Preparation

  • Hull strawberries and discard green leaves.
  • Place all ingredients in a food processor and blend until smooth.

Serving Suggestions

Milk shake can be served with whipped cream, marshmallows and if you want texture, add toasted flaked almonds.
