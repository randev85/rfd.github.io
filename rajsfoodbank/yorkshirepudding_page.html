Yorkshire Puddings

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Plain Flour 140g
Medium Eggs 4x1
Whole Milk 200ml
Salt 1 tsp
Vegetable Oil 160ml

Yield: 8 yorkshire puddings

Preparation

  • Sift flour and salt into a bowl.
  • Whisk eggs and milk in a bowl.
  • Make a well in the middle of the flour and gradually mix in the milk and the egg. Do this slowly to ensure a smooth batter. Pour batter into jug.
  • Pre-heat oven to 205’C.
  • Using 2xfour mould Yorkshire pudding baking trays, add 20ml of vegetable oil to each mould. Place trays in the pre-heated oven for 6 minutes.

Cooking Instructions

  • Remove tray oven, close door to retain heat, add roughly 50-60ml of batter to each mould and return tray to oven. Do this as quickly and safely as possible to stop heat loss. Reduce heat in the oven to 190’C and cook for 12-15 minutes until golden brown and 4-5cm in height.

Serving Suggestions

Yorkshire puddings go well with roast dinners.

Storing instructions

consume when made.
