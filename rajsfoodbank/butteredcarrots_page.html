Buttered Carrots

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Carrot 400g
Unsalted Butter 60g
Soft Brown Sugar 15g
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 4 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and cut carrot in half before diagonally slicing to 5mm thickness.

Cooking Instructions

  • Add slice carrots to sauce pan and submerge with water.
  • Place sauce pan over a medium heat and add sugar, butter and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  • Bring to the boil and reduce to the simmer 8-10 minutes. Once liquid has evaporated and carrots are glazed, remove from heat and serve .

Serving Suggestions

serve alongside a roast dinner.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and store in an airtight contain before placing in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.

Reheating Instructions

Place in microwave for 2-3 minutes.
