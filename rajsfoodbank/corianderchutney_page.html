Mint & Coriander Chutney

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Fresh Coriander 100g
Green Chilli 1x1
Mint 100g
Lime 1x1
Caster Sugar ½tsp
Vegetable Oil 20ml
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 240g

Preparation

  • Pick mint leaves.
  • Tear coriander leaves from stalks and discard stalks.
  • Remove tops from chillies.
  • Juice and zest lime.
  • Put mint, coriander, chillies, lime zest, lime juice and sugar in a food processor. Blend and pour vegetable oil in slowly before seasoning to taste.

Serving Suggestions

Mint and coriander chutney can be used as a dip for kebabs and samosa .

Storing instructions

Consume within 1 week of making.
