Chicken & Avocado Quinoa

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breast 2 Fillets
Quinoa 200g
Vegetable Stock 250ml
Lime 1½x1
Red Wine Vinegar 20ml
Honey 1 tsp
Fresh Coriander 60g
Avocado 1x1
Red Bell Pepper 1x1
Red Onion 1x1
Olive Oil 90ml
Cracked Black Pepper ½ tsp
Maldon Sea Salt 1 tsp

Yield: 4-6 portions

Preparation

  • Cut chicken into 1cm thick slices.
  • Pick and finely slice coriander leaves.
  • Zest and juice limes.
  • Deseed and peel avocado before dicing into 1cm cubes and coating with lime juice.
  • Deseed and dice red pepper.
  • Peel and finely dice red onion.

Cooking Instructions

  • Pace a saucepan over a high heat before adding quinoa and vegetable stock. Bring to the boil and reduce to the simmer for 12-14 minutes until cornels have come out of quinoa.
  • Whilst quinoa is cooking, place a frying pan over a medium/high heat and add 30ml of olive oil. Add sliced chicken to the pan, season with salt and pepper before cooking for 2-3 minutes each side until golden brown. Remove from heat and allow to cool to room tempreture.
  • Once quinoa is cooked, drain water and allow to cool to room temperature.
  • Mix quinoa with chicken, coriander, lime zest, diced avocado, red onion, red pepper, honey, red wine vinegar and season with salt and pepper.
