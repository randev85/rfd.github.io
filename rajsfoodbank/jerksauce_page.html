Jerk Sauce

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Scotch Bonnet Pepper 1x1
Red Pepper ½x1
Garlic Cloves 6 Cloves
Root Ginger 2 Inches
Spring Onions 6 Stalks
Ground Allspice 1 tsp
Black Peppercorns 10x1
Brown Sugar 45g
Orange 1x1
Vegetable Oil 25ml
Dark Soy Sauce 60ml
Dried Thyme 10g
Cloves 1x1
Bay leaf 1x1
Ground Cinnamon ½ tsp
Honey 20g
Tomato Ketchup 20g
Coriander 40g
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 150ml

Preparation

  • Deseed red peppers.
  • Peel a crush garlic cloves.
  • Peel root ginger.
  • Roughly chop spring onions.
  • Juice orange.
  • Put scotch bonnet, red pepper, garlic, ginger, spring onions, allspice, 1 teaspoon of salt, peppercorns, brown sugar, orange juice, soy sauce, dried thyme, bay leaf and ground cinnamon in food processor and blend. Pour vegetable oil in until smooth paste is made.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a saucepan on a medium/high heat and add Jerk paste.
  • Regularly stir and cook for 8-10 minutes.
  • Add honey, ketchup and 100ml of water before bringing to the boil and reducing to the simmer.
  • Season to taste.

Serving Suggestions

Jerk sauce compliments Caribbean food.

Storing instructions

Allow jus to cool to room temperature before putting in an air-tight container and placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 2 weeks of making it
