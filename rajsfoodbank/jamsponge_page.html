Jam Sponge

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Self-raising Flour 225g
Unsalted Butter 225g
Caster Sugar 225g
Medium Eggs 4x1
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Strawberry Jam 150g
Desiccated Coconut 100g

Yield: 8-10 portions

Preparation

  • Sift flour and baking powder in bowl.
  • Place butter in the microwave for 20-30 second until it’s soft but not melted.
  • Use an electric whisk to cream sugar and butter together. Whisk until butter is lighter in complexion.
  • Line a sandwich baking tin with grease proof paper.
  • Add one egg at a time to the creamed butter/sugar until all eggs are combined.
  • Add flour to bowl and whisk in on a slow setting to begin with. Increase speed once flours is combined and mix for 1-2 minutes.
  • Add cake batter to sandwich tin and spread out evenly.
  • Pre-heat oven to 165’C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place sandwich tin in the oven for 20-25 minutes. Use a skewer to check if the centre is cooked
  • Allow the cake to cool and remove from sandwich tin.
  • Spread strawberry jam over the top of cake.
  • Finally, sprinkle over desiccated coconut before slicing out portions.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with ice cream or custard.

Storing instructions

Cover with clingfilm and place in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.

Reheating Instructions

Place in microwave for 1-2 minutes.
