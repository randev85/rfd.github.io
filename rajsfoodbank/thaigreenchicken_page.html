Thai Green Chicken Curry

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Olive Oil 30ml
White Onion 2x1
Garlic Cloves 2 Cloves
Chicken Breast 4 Fillets
Lemon Grass 5 Stems
Root Ginger 1 Inch
Coconut Milk 1x400g
Chicken Stock 150ml
Thai Green Curry Paste 24g
Thai Fish Sauce 40ml
Fresh Coriander 50g
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 4 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and finely dice white onion.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Cut chicken breast into 1 inch cubes.
  • Finely chop lemon grass.
  • Peel and grate root ginger.
  • Pick and finely chop coriander leaves.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a medium sized wok over a medium/high heat and vegetable oil.
  • Once oil starts to smoke, add cubes of chicken and brown.
  • Cook chicken for 4 minutes before adding onions and garlic. cook for 3 minutes.
  • Add lemon grass, grated ginger, coconut milk, chicken stock and curry paste. Stir and cook for 3 minutes.
  • Add fish sauce, soy sauce and chopped coriander. Bring to the boil and reduce to the simmer for 20 minutes.
  • Season to taste before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Thai green curry goes well with freshly cooked jasmine rice.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave and heat for 2-3 minutes.
