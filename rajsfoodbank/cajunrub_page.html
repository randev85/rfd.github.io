Cajun Seasoning

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Paprika 60g
Cayenne Pepper 60g
Black Pepper Corns 30g
Garlic Powder 30g
Onion Powder 90g
Dried Oregano 60g
salt 8g

Yield: 340g

Preparation

  • Mix all ingredients together.

Serving Suggestions

Marinade your meat and either grill and BBQ.

Storing instructions

Store in an airtight jar and store in the fridge. Consume within 7 days of making it.
