French Onion Soup

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted Butter 125g
Olive Oil 40ml
White Onion 2.5kg
Caster Sugar 20g
Garlic Cloves 6 Cloves
Plain Flour 75g
Dry White Wine 750ml
Beef Stock 2 Litres
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 10-12 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and slice white onion.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a large sauce pan over a low/medium heat and add the vegetable oil.
  • Add the sliced onion and butter before covering with a lid. Cook for 10 minutes or until onions are soft.
  • Remove lid and add sugar. Cook for a further 20 minutes whilst continually stirring.
  • Once the onions are caramel in colour, add garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.
  • Add the flour and stir the mix ensuring even coverage.
  • Turn the heat up to high and add the wine and beef stock. Cover, bring to the boil and reduce heat to simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  • Season to taste.

Serving Suggestions

The sweetness of this soup goes well with the saltiness of cheese toast.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature before placing in an airtight container and placing in the fridge. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Gently bring back up to temperature in saucepan on stove. Alternatively, warm in microwave for 3 minutes, stirring every 1 minute to ensure even warming.
