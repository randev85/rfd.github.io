Cauliflower & Lentil Curry

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable Oil 30ml
White Onion 2x1
Garlic Clove 2 Cloves
Root Ginger 1 inch
Ground Coriander 2 tsp
Ground Cumin 2 tsp
Turmeric Powder ½ tsp
Yellow Lentils 100g
Vegetable Stock 200ml
Cauliflower 1 head
Coconut Milk 1x400g
Coriander 30g
Lemon 1x1
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and finely slice white onions.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Peel and grate root ginger.
  • Soak lentils for 2 hours and rinse with running cold water.
  • Cut cauliflower into 1 inch florets.
  • Pick coriander leaves.
  • Juice lemon.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a saucepan over a medium/high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Add sliced onions and sauté for 10 minutes whilst occasionally stirring.
  • Add garlic, ginger, ground coriander, cumin and turmeric before cooking and stirring for 2 minutes.
  • Add lentil and vegetable stock, bring to the boil and reduce to the simmer for 15-18 minutes.
  • In a separate frying pan, place over a medium/high heat and add 1 tbsp of vegetable oil. Add cauliflower florets to the pan and brown evenly
  • Add florets and coconut milk to lentils Cook for a further 10 minutes.
  • Add lemon juice and coriander before seasoning with salt and pepper.

Serving Suggestions

This dish goes great with naan bread, roti or pilaf rice.

Storing instructions

Place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave for 2-3 minutes.
