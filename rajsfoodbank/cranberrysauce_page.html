Cranberry Sauce

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Muscovado Sugar 100g
Orange Juice 100ml
Frozen Cranberries 250g
Table Salt Pinch

Yield: 400g

Cooking Instructions

  • Place saucepan on medium/high heat and add orange juice and sugar.
  • Bring to the boil and add frozen cranberries. Reduce heat to medium and cook for 8-10 minutes until sauce holds itâ€™s shape.
  • Season to taste.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with roast turkey.

Storing instructions

Cool down and place in an air tight container in the refrigerator. Consume with 1 month of making.
