Poached Egg

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Medium Egg 2x1
White Wine Vinegar 80ml
Table Salt 3 tsp

Yield: 2 eggs

Cooking Instructions

  • Place 2 litres of water in a saucepan over a high heat and bring to the boil.
  • Once water is boiling, add white wine vinegar and salt and reduce heat to low/medium.
  • Carefully and slowly crack egg into water. Cook for 3-4 minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Poached eggs are normally enjoy with a bready carb.
