Sausage Roll

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Puff Pastry Sheet 1x320g
Sausage Meat 250g
White Onion 1x1
Sage Leaves 10g
Medium Egg 1x1
Salt & Pepper To Season

Yield: 6 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and finely dice white onion.
  • Finely dice sage leafs.
  • Cut puff pastry into 6 equal rectangles.
  • Crack and whisk egg.
  • Pre-heat oven to 170â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a medium sized pan over a medium/high heat.
  • Add oil, diced white onion and sage to pan. Cook until onions have softened.
  • Mix sausage meat, onions and sage before seasoning with a pinch of salt and pepper. Put mixture into piping bag and cut a hole at the end of the tip of the bag that has a ¾ diameter. Pipe mixture length ways to the right of the centre of each rectangle of puff pastry. Fold pastry over cylinder and glue with egg wash. Crimp edge with fork and cut with knife to create a straight edge. Make 3 diagonal incisions across the top of the pastry and brush surface with egg wash.
  • Repeat process until all sausage rolls are compiled before placing on a grease proofed baking tray and placing in the oven for 15-20 minutes.

Storing instructions

Consume when made.
