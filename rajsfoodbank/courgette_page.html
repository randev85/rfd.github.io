Courgette

Soil Requirements

Courgettes require a neutral soil of between 6.0 to 7.5 ph. Test your soil with a soil ph meter and if required, add alkaline by either using lime, mulches or a bicarbonate of soda solution (1 tsp per 2 litres of water). Alternatively, if there is large concentrations of alkaline in the soil, treat with a vinegar solution (1 tablespoon per 2 litres) until ph levels are right.

Courgettes also require semi loose soil that is free from weeds and roots. This is because the plant needs good drainage.

Space Requirements

Area 40cm²
Height 30cm

Additional Information

Once the flowers begin to fruit, it is important to support the stems with rigid sticks to support the weight.

Seed Germination & Planting

Sow courgette seeds indoors at the start of April. Plant seeds in compost filled cell trays, at a depth of 1cm. Water cell tray well and place tray inside a propagator. Sustain temperature at between 18-21â€™C.

Seedling can be transferred outside as early as the middle of May. The ground will have to be prepared two weeks in advance of this. This is done by mixing in a 5cm layer of compost with the soil and, by creating planting pockets 90cm apart from each other. Ensure the spot you choose gets direct sunlight for the duration of the day. Transfer plant to ground and pat soil around it before watering in well.

Water Requirements

Keep the soil constantly moist by watering around the plants, not over them.

Feed with liquid fertiliser every 10-14 days. Once the plant has started to fruit, Feed with high potash liquid fertiliser.

Pruning Requirements

Remove any yellow or dying leaves.

Once courgette plants have begun to fruit between four and six courgettes, you can begin pruning plant. Start by snipping off the tips and continue pruning plants as needed throughout the growing season.

Yeild:

8-10 courgettes per plant.

Harvesting Guidelines

Harvest fruit once they are between 10-12cm long.

Storing Guidlines

Once picked, store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place for about 2 months.
