Granola

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable Oil 30ml
Honey 155ml
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Rolled Oats 450g
Sunflower Seeds 75g
Pumpkin Seeds 75g
Flaked Almonds 100g

Yeild: 18-20 Portions

Preparation

  • Pre-heat oven to 160â€™C
  • Mix all ingredients in bowl thoroughly
  • Line 2 baking trays with grease-proof paper and spread granola mixture over each equally

Cooking Instructions

  • Place trays in oven for 15 minutes before swapping positions and cooking for a further 15 minutes.
  • Remove trays from oven and allow to cool.
  • Break granola into bitesize pieces.

Serving Suggestions

Granola is normally served with milk, but can also be served with yoghurt, compote and fresh fruit.

Storing instructions

Ensure Granola is dry before storing in air tight container. Store for no longer than 2 weeks.
