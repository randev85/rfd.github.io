Rice Pudding

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Pudding Rice 200g
Evaporated Milk 400ml
Whole Milk 800ml
Caster Sugar 50g
Ground Nutmeg ¼ tsp
Ground Cinnamon 1 tsp
Unsalted Butter 25g
Vanilla Essence 1 tsp

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Place pudding rice, sugar, nutmeg and cinnamon in a deep baking dish and cover with milk, evaporated milk and vanilla essence. Place lumps of butter evenly around the dish
  • Pre-heat oven to 150â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Carefully place baking dish in the centre of the oven and cook for 2-2½ hours. Stir after 1 hour.
  • Remove rice pudding from the oven and it should have golden brown topping.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with honey or jam.

Storing instructions

Cover with clingfilm and place in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave for 1-2 minutes.
