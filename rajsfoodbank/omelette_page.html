Omelette

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Medium Egg 2x1
Vegetabl Oil 20ml
Salt & Pepper To Season

Yield: 1 portion

Preparation

  • Crack eggs in a bowl and whisk before seasoning with a pinch of salt and pepper.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a frying pan over a medium/high heat.
  • Add vegetable oil to the frying pan and distribute evenly.
  • Once vegetable oil is hot, pour egg mixture into pan and allow to fluff up.
  • Pull egg from the side to the centre of the pan and allow runny egg to fill the void.
  • Repeat this until there is very little runny egg.
  • Fill half the circle with desired ingredients and fold over omellete.
  • Reduce heat to low and cook for 1 minute each side before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Omelettes can be filled with an array of ingredients. My favourite fillings include, cheese, spring onion, tomatoes, ham and peppers.
