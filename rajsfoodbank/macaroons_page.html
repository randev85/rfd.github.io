Macaroons

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Medium Eggs 6x1
Caster Sugar 100g
Icing Sugar 400g
Ground Almond Powder 220g

Yield: 24-32 portions

Preparation

  • Clean Mixing Bowl surface with white wine vinegar and clean cloth.
  • Separate egg whites from yolks and discard yolks.
  • Using an electric stand mixer and the whisk attachment, whisk egg whites until they start to foam.
  • Add Caster sugar a tablespoon at a time and whisk until mixture becomes glossy, fluffy and holds a soft peak.
  • Sift Icing sugar and ground almond in a separate bowl. Add almond and sugar to egg soft peak egg and quickly incorporate with 20-30 folding motions of a rubber spatula.
  • Line baking tray with a silicone non-stick mat.
  • Spoon small amount of mixture into piping bag, cut tip off bag creating a 1cm diameter and pipe a small disc on to silicone mat. If disc holds a peak, then fold mixture a few more times with the rubber spatula and retest until it flattens immediately when piped.
  • Transfer remaining mixture to piping bag and pipe onto silicone mat making sure the diameter of each macaroon is about 3cm in diameter. Leave a 1cm perimeter are each macaroon.
  • Leave macaroons out at room temperature for about an hour until macaroon has formed hard skin on it’s surface.
  • Pre-heat oven to 140’C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Bake macaroons for 10 minutes until they set. Be careful not to brown.
>

Storing instructions

Store in air-tight container. Consume with 1 week of making.
