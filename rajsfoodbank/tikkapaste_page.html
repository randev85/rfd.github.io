Tikka Masala Paste

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Coriander Seeds 30g
Cumin Seeds 30g
Garlic Powder 25g
Paprika 30g
Garam Masala 15g
Ground Ginger 15g
Chilli Powder 10g
Ground Turmeric 2.5g
Chilli Powder 10g
Dried Mint 15g
Table Salt 10g
Lemon Juice 5ml
White Wine Vinegar 150ml
Vegetable Oil 150ml

Yield: 300g

Preparation

  • Grind coriander seeds and cumin seeds in a pestle and mortar.
  • Combined all the dry ingredients and stir well.
  • Add lemon juice, vinegar and 30ml of water to make a paste.

Cooking Instructions

  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan and add spice paste before cooking for 10 minute.
  • Once oil rises to the surface, the paste is cooked.

Serving Suggestions

The tikka paste can be used as a base for a curry or and marinade for grilled meats.

Storing instructions

Once cool, store in an airtight jar and place in fridge. Consume within 1 month of making it.
