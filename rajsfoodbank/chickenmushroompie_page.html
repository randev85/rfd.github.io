Chicken & Mushroom Pie

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breast 3 fillets
White Onion 1x1
Leek 1x1
Button Mushrooms 200g
Whole Milk 400ml
Vegetable Oil 30ml
Unsalted Butter 50g
Plain Flour 50g
Dried Thyme ½ tsp
Salt & Pepper To Taste
Puff Pastry 1x320g
Medium Egg 1x1

Yield: 8-10 portions

Preparation

  • Cut chicken in 1 inch cubes.
  • Peel and slice white onion.
  • Wash and slice leeks.
  • Cut button mushrooms in half.
  • Crack and whisk egg.
  • Cut puff pastry sheet in 8 equal pieces and place on grease proofed baking tray.
  • Brush puff pastry with egg wash.
  • Pre-heat oven to 175â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place puff pastry in oven for 10-12 minutes until golden brown .
  • Place a medium sized saucepan of a medium/high heat and add half of the vegetable oil. .
  • Add diced chicken to sauce and brown before setting aside.
  • Add the remaining vegetable oil followed by the sliced onions, sliced leek and button mushrooms. Fry for 5-8 minutes until soft.
  • Add butter, thyme and flour to the vegetables and stir to ensure an even coating.
  • Add chicken back to the pan and pour in milk. Whilst sauce heats an thickens, stir continually to ensure sauce is smooth.
  • Reduce heat and cook for 8 minutes or until chicken is fully cooked. Season to taste.
  • Add filling to deep dish and place puff pastry squares on top.

Serving Suggestions

Chicken and mushroom pie goes great with mash potato and steamed vegetables.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave and heat foe 2-3 minutes.
