French Toast

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Medium Eggs 3x1
Whole Milk 80ml
Cinnamon Powder 1 tsp
Caster Sugar 2 tsp
Sliced White Bread 4 Slices
Table Salt ½ tsp
Vegetable Oil 60ml

Yield: Serves 4

Preparation

  • Whisk eggs, milk, cinnamon, sugar and salt in a bowl before pouring into a deep, flat baking dish.
  • Add sliced bread to the baking dish and allow to soak in egg mixture thoroughly on both sides.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place large frying pan over a medium heat.
  • Add 30ml of vegetable oil to the pan and distribute evenly.
  • Remove sliced bread from egg mixture and shake off any excess liquid before placing in frying Pan.
  • Fry for 2-3 minutes before flipping.
  • Only flip French Toast once bottom side is golden brown.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes on other side before serving.
  • Repeat process for second batch.

Serving Suggestions

French toast goes well with fresh strawberries, raspberries, and blue berries, fresh cream, maple syrup, golden syrup, bananas, toffee sauce and even bacon

Storing instructions

Allow French Toast to come down to room temperature before putting in an air-tight container and placing in a refrigerator. This recipe contains Egg and should be kept for no longer than 4 days.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave and warm for 1 minute.
