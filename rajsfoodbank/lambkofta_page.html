Lamb Kofta

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable Oil 60ml
Minced Lamb 500g
Ground Cumin 1 tsp
Ground coriander 2 tsp
Garlic Cloves 3 Cloves
Mint 40g
Slat & Pepper To Season

Yield: 8 portions

Preparation

  • Place 8 wooden skewers in water and soak for 2 hours.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Pick and finely chop mint leaves.
  • Thoroughly mash and mix ingredients in bowl before seasoning with 1 teaspoon of salt and a pinch of pepper.
  • Divide lamb mixture into 8 equal portions and roll each portion into 3 inch sausage shapes. Use warm water to ensure kofta surface is smooth.
  • Skewer each kofta, place on a tray and in the refrigerator for 30-60 minutes.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a frying pan on a medium heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Once Oil is hot, carefully place koftas in pan a fry for 4 minutes each quarter rotation. Ensure side is browned and sealed thoroughly before rotating.

Serving Suggestions

Lamb kofta goes great with flat bread and mint yoghurt.

Storing instructions

Allow koftas to cool to room temperature before putting in an air-tight container and placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave for 2-3 minutes until piping hot.
