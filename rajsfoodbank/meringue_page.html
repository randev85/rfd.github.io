Meringue

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Large Eggs 4x1
Caster Sugar 115g
Icing Sugar 115g

Yield: 24 portions

Preparation

  • Separate egg whites form yolks and discard yolks.
  • Clean mixing bowl with white wine vinegar and clean cloth.
  • Add whites to bowl and whisk on a medium speed until mixture resembles fluffy consistency.
  • Increase speed and add caster sugar 1 tablespoon at a time.
  • Whisk until mixture become thick and glossy. This should take 2-3 minutes.
  • Sift icing sugar in a separate bowl.
  • Add icing sugar a third at a time and fold into mixture gently.
  • Line 2 baking tray with greaseproof paper.
  • Transfer meringue mixture to piping bag with star nozzle and pipe out individual blobs that have 2-3cm diameter and a perimeter 2 cm.
  • Pre-heat oven to 100â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place trays in oven for 1½-1¾ hours .
  • Cool on wire rack.

Storing instructions

Store in air-tight container. Consume with 1 week of making.
