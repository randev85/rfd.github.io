Pavlova

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Large Eggs 4x1
Caster Sugar 250g
White Wine Vinegar 1 tsp
Cornflour 1 tsp
Vanilla Essence 1 tsp
Strawberries 500g
Raspberries 200g
Icing Sugar 45g
Double Cream 350ml

Yield: 8-10 portions

Preparation

  • Line a baking tray with grease proof paper and stencil 2 circles that are 20cm in diameter.
  • Separate egg whites from yolks and use an electric whisk to mix whites to a soft peak. Continue to whisk whilst adding a tablespoon of caster sugar at a time. Once all of the sugar is combined, meringue should look glossy.
  • Whisk in vinegar, cornflour and vanilla essence until meringue is smooth to touch.
  • Transfer to a piping bag and pipe meringue within stencil on grease proof paper.
  • dehull and cut strawberries in half.
  • Use an electric whisk to mix double cream with 30g of icing sugar. Whisk until cream holds a soft peak.
  • Pre-heat oven to 150â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place meringues in the oven for 1 hour. After an hour, turn off oven and leave meringues to dry out inside for 20-30 minutes.
  • Remove first disc of pavlova from baking tray and place on serving plate. Spread half the cream on top of it and place the second pavlova on top of that
  • Add the remaining cream on top of the second pavlova and top with strawberries and raspberries.
  • Finish by dusting with remaining icing sugar.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with a cup of tea.

Storing instructions

Cover with clingfilm and place in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
