Naan Bread

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Strong White Flour 500g
Caster Sugar 20g
Table Salt 1 tsp
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Whole Milk 250ml
Vegetable Oil 50ml

Yield: 10 Naans

Preparation

  • Sift flour, salt, sugar and baking powder.
  • Mix Milk and 30ml of Vegetable Oil.
  • Make a well in the middle of the flour and gradually add milk.
  • Mix flour into milk until dough comes away from the side of the bowl.
  • Tip dough on to floured work surface and knead for 8-10 minutes until dough become elastic.
  • Put dough into oiled bowl, clingfilm to prove for 10-15 minutes in a warm place.
  • Line a baking tray with greaseproof paper.
  • Tip dough on to an oiled surface and roll dough into long sausage shape before dividing into 10 equal pieces.
  • Using a rolling pin, roll out dough pieces and place on baking tray.
  • Turn grill on to maximum.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place tray under grill and cook Naan bread for 2-3 minute on either side.

Serving Suggestions

Naan bread is a great accompaniment with traditional Indian curries.

Storing instructions

Once cool, Place in an air tight container in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making.
