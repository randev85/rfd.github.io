Salsa Verde

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Tarragon 100g
Parsley 100g
Garlic Cloves 2 Cloves
Dijon Mustard 3 tsp
Capers 40g
Olive Oil 200ml
Sherry Vinegar 30ml
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 480g

Preparation

  • Pick parsley leaves.
  • Pick tarragon leaves.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Put tarragon, parsley, garlic, Dijon mustard, capers and sherry vinegar in a food processor. Blend whilst drizzling in olive oil.
  • Season to taste.

Serving Suggestions

Salsa Verde goes great with steamed white fish.

Storing instructions

Consume within 1 week of making.
