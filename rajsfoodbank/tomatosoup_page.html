Tomato & Basil Soup

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Olive oil 75ml
White Onion 5x1
Carrots 2x1
Garlic Cloves 4 Cloves
Tinned Tomatoes 3x400g
Vegetable Stock 2.5 Litres
Caster Sugar 3 tsp
Dried Oregano 1 tsp
Fresh Basil 60g
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and slice white onion.
  • Peel and slice Carrots.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Pick and finely slice Basil leaves.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a large saucepan over a medium/high heat and add the vegetable oil.
  • Add the sliced onion and carrots. Cook for 10 minutes or until vegetables are soft.
  • Add crushed garlic and dried oregano and cook for 3 minutes.
  • Add tinned tomatoes and vegetable stock, bring to the boil and reduce to the simmer for 30 minutes.
  • Remove from the heat and blend by either using a food processor or stick blender.
  • Pass soup through a chinois to remove any large pieces and return to saucepan.
  • Adjust consistency by adding water if needed, season to taste and add a garnish of fresh basil leaves.

Serving Suggestions

This soup goes well with crusty bread and lots of butter.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature before placing in an airtight container and placing in the fridge. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Gently bring back up to temperature in saucepan on stove. Alternatively, warm in microwave for 3 minutes, stirring every 1 minute to ensure even warming.
