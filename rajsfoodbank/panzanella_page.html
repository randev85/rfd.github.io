Panzanella Salad

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Ciabatta Bread 180g
Cherry Tomatoes 2 Punnets
Capers 80g
Red Onion 1x1
Marinated Anchovy Fillet 8 Fillets
Basil 80g
Parsley 50g
Olive Oil 60ml
Red Wine Vinegar 20ml
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 4-6 portions

Preparation

  • Cut Ciabatta into 1 inch Cubes.
  • Cut cherry tomatoes in half.
  • Roughly chop capers.
  • Peel and finely dice red onion.
  • Roughly chop anchovy fillets.
  • Pick basil leaves.
  • Pick and finely chop parsley leaves.
  • Whisk olive oil with red wine vinegar and season with salt and pepper.
  • Place cubed ciabatta on baking tray lined with grease proof paper.
  • Pre-heat oven to 150â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place ciabatta in the oven for 6-8 minutes until bread is dry.
  • Mix ciabatta crotons with basil leaves, cherry tomatoes, red onion, capers, parsley and anchovies. Dress salad with vinaigrette and serve.

Serving Suggestions

Serve by itself.

Storing instructions

Store in an airtight contain before placing in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
