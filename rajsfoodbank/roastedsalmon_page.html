Roasted Salmon

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Salmon Fillet 1kg
Vegetable Oil 30ml
Lemon 1x1
Maldon Sea Salt 1 tsp
Cracked Black Pepper 1 tsp
Dill 10g

Yield: 4-5 portions

Preparation

  • Debone salmon fillet and descale salmon skin. Cut fillet into 5 smaller fillets using a sharp knife.
  • Zest lemon.
  • Finely chop dill leaves.
  • Mix vegetable oil with lemon zest and chopped dill.
  • Line baking try with greaseproof paper.
  • Brush salmon fillets with dill vegetable oil and place on baking tray. Sprinkle with Maldon sea salt and cracked black pepper.
  • Pre-heat oven to 190â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place tray in oven and roast for 10-12 minutes .

Serving Suggestions

Roasted salmon goes great with crushed new potatoes, steamed French beans and a Greek yoghurt dip.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature and store in air-tight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume with 4 days of making.

Reheating Instructions

Place in microwave for 1-2 muntes.
