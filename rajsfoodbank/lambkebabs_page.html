Lamb Kebabs

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Boneless Shoulder of Lamb 1.5kg
Olive Oil 100ml
Red Wine 100ml
Dried Oregano 10g
Lemon 2x1
Garlic Cloves 2x1
Salt & Pepper To Season

Yield: 4-6 portions

Preparation

  • Trim and cut shoulder of lamb into 1 inch chunks.
  • Zest and juice both lemons.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Place lamb chunks in a sealed polyethene bag and add olive oil, red wine, oregano, garlic, lemon zest and juice. Place in the refrigerator for 24 hours.
  • Soak wooden skewers in water for 2 hours.
  • Remove lamb from marinade and thread on to skewers.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a griddle pan on a medium/high heat.
  • Season kebabs with salt and pepper.
  • Brush oil over the griddle pan and once oil starts to smoke, place kebabs on griddle.
  • Cook kebabs foe 10-12 minutes whilst regularly rotating skewers.
  • Rest for 2 minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Lamb kebabs go great with flatbread, shredded red cabbage and cucumber yoghurt.

Storing instructions

Allow roasted leg of lamb to cool to room temperature before putting in an air-tight container and placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave for 2-3 minutes until piping hot.
