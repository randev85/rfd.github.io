Chocolate Eclairs

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted Butter 100g
Plain Flour 150g
Caster Sugar 1 tsp
Table Salt 1 tsp
Medium Egg 4x1
Milk Chocolate 200g
Double Cream 500ml
Vanilla Essence 2 tsp
Icing Sugar 100g

Yield: 20 small portions

Preparation

  • Cut butter into cubes.
  • Sift flour, caster sugar and table salt into a bowl.
  • Whisk vanilla essence and icing sugar into double cream until cream thickens and medium peaks are achieved. Clingfilm and place in the refrigerator until needed.
  • Break chocolate into small pieces.
  • Crack and whisk eggs.
  • Line baking tray with greaseproof paper.
  • Pre-heat oven to 200’C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place 250ml of water and cubed butter in saucepan over a high heat.
  • Once water and butter have come to the boil, remove pan from the heat, add all of the flour and mix until paste is achieved. Allow to cool to room temperature.
  • Add whisked eggs gradually in 4 stages whilst thoroughly mixing with the paste.
  • Texture of choux pastry should be loose and still be able to coat the back of a spoon without losing shape.
  • Place choux pastry in piping bag that has a 1cm diameter nozzle.
  • Pipe choux pastry on to baking tray with a length of 3cm each. Leave an inch perimeter around each piped line.
  • Place baking tray in the oven, close oven door and reduce heat to 180’C. Cook for 15-18 minutes.
  • Place broken chocolate in glass bowl and place glass bowl over hot simmering saucepan of water. Occasionally mix until all chocolate has melted.
  • Remove pastry shells out of the oven and cool on wire rack.
  • Transfer cream to piping bag with a 1cm diameter nozzle.
  • Cut pastries in half, coat one side with melted chocolate and place on a tray. Repeat this until all pastries are covered. Leave at room temperature until chocolate has solidified.
  • Carefully pipe the centre of each pastry with cream and be careful not to leave any finger prints on the chocolate.

Storing instructions

Store in refrigerator. Consume with 1 week of making.
