Carrot & Quinoa Tabbouleh

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Spring Onions 3 Stalks
Quinoa 350g
Carrot 1x1
Garlic Clove 1 Clove
Cumin Powder 1 tsp
Smoked Paprika ½ tsp
Fresh Coriander 60g
Fresh Parsley 60g
Fresh Mint 60g
Jalapeno Pepper 1x1
Olive Oil 60ml
Cracked Black Pepper ½ tsp
Maldon Sea Salt 1 tsp

Yield: 3-4 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and grate carrot.
  • Pick and finely slice coriander leaves.
  • Pick and finely slice parsley leaves.
  • Pick and finely slice mint leaves.
  • Finely slice jalapeno pepper.
  • Finely slice spring onion.
  • Peel and crush garlic clove.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a saucepan over a high heat and add quinoa and 420ml of water. Bring to the boil and reduce to the simmer for 12-14 minutes until cornels have come out of quinoa.
  • Once quinoa is cooked, drain water and allow to cool to room temperature.
  • Mix quinoa with carrot, spring onion, garlic, cumin powder, smoked paprika, coriander, mint, parsley, jalapeno pepper and oil before seasoning with salt and pepper.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with tuna steak.

Storing instructions

Store in an airtight contain before placing in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
