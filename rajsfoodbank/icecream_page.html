Vanilla Ice Cream

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Medium Eggs 8x1
Caster Sugar 200g
Corn Flour 2 tsp
Double Cream 600ml
Whole Milk 600ml
Vanilla Pods 2

Yield: 10-12 portions

Preparation

  • Separate egg yolks from egg whites and discard whites.
  • Use an electric whisk to mix egg yolks, caster sugar and corn flour. Mix until thick and pale in complexion.
  • Cut vanilla pods in half and scrap out seeds.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place cream, milk and vanilla pod seeds in a sauce pan. Gently heat over a low/medium flame and stir regularly to stop milk from catching at the bottom of the pan.
  • Once milk is starting to evaporate, remove from the heat and slowly pour, whilst whisking, into the egg and sugar mix.
  • Pass this liquid through a chinois and into a clean saucepan.
  • Place saucepan over a low heat and regularly stir for about 10 minutes. Be sure to stir the sides to stop mixture catching. Once mixture has thickened enough to coat the back of a wooden spoon, remove from the heat and transfer to a clean bowl.
  • Allow mixture to cool to room temperature and be sure to stir occasionally to stop a skin forming.
  • Once cool, Transfer mixture to an ice cream churner, and churn for 30 minutes- 1 hour. Transfer ice cream to plastic box and place in the freezer.

Serving Suggestions

Serve in a cone.

Storing instructions

Cover with clingfilm and place in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
