Lentil & Spinach Soup

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted Butter 50g
White Onion 5x1
Garlic Cloves 5 Cloves
Tomatoes 5x1
Puy Lentils 500g
Vegetable Stock 750ml
Spinach Leaves 500g
Parsley 60g
Basil 60g
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 10-12 portions

Preparation

  • Soak puy lentils for 24 hours in cold water.
  • Wash puy lentil twice through with cold water.
  • Peel and slice white onions.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Roughly chop tomatoes.
  • Wash spinach twice and spin dry.
  • Pick and finely chop parsley.
  • Pick and finely chop basil.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a large saucepan over a medium/high heat and add the vegetable oil.
  • Add the sliced onion and sweat on a low heat for 10 minutes.
  • Add crushed garlic and cook for a further 2 minutes.
  • Add tomatoes, puy lentils and vegetable stock before bringing to the boil and reducing to the simmer.
  • After 40 minutes, add spinach leaves and cook for a further 2 minutes.
  • Transfer ingredients to a food processor, or alternatively, blend with a hand blender.
  • Pass soup through a chinois to ensure smooth consistency before returning to sauce pan.
  • Add parsley, basil and season to taste.

Serving Suggestions

The soup goes well with crusty bread and lots of butter or with naan bread.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature before placing in an airtight container and placing in the fridge. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Gently bring back up to temperature in saucepan on stove. Alternatively, warm in microwave for 3 minutes, stirring every 1 minute to ensure even warming.
