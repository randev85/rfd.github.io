Quorn Paella

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Olive Oil 75ml
Quorn Pieces 1kg
White Onion 1x1
Garlic Cloves 2 Cloves
Saffron ½ tsp
Smoked Paprika 1 tsp
Paella Rice 300g
Vegetable Stock 850ml
Garden Peas 200g
Lime 1x1
Red Chilli ½ tsp
Spring Onion 1 stalk
Red Pepper 1x1
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 4-6 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and finely dice white onion.
  • Deseed and cut red pepper into 1cm cubes.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Finely slice red chilli.
  • Finely slice spring onion.
  • Cut lime into 8 equal wedges.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a large saucepan on a medium/heat and add ½ of the vegetable oil.
  • Once the oil is hot enough, brown quorn pieces in the pan. Remove and set aside.
  • Add remaining oil to pan and fry onions and peppers for 3 minutes.
  • Add saffron and smoked paprika and cook out spices for 2 minutes.
  • Add garlic and sauté for another 2 minutes.
  • Now pour in the paella rice and stir continually to ensure the rice has an even coating of oil.
  • Add 2 ladles of vegetable stock to the pan so that the paella rice is submerged and turn the heat down to medium. When the paella has absorbed the vegetable stock, add more and repeat this process until the paella is al dente. If you have run out of stock, use water. Remember to stir the rice continually to stop it catching at the bottom of the pan.
  • Add quorn and peas before cooking for 5-6 minutes before seasoning to taste.
  • Serve the paella in a big dish and garnish with lime wedges, sliced spring onions and sliced red chilli.

Serving Suggestions

I like to serve Paella with homemade garlic bread, a light salad and a glass of white wine.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave and heat for 3-4 minutes.
