Potato Wedges

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Maris Piper Potato 500g
Vegetable Oil 30ml
Cajun Spice 2 tsp
Maldon Sea Salt 1½ tsp
Garlic Powder 1 tsp
Cracked Black Pepper 1 tsp

Yield: 4-6 portions

Preparation

  • Wash and cut Maris piper potato into equal wedges.
  • Mix vegetable oil, cajun spice, Maldon sea salt, garlic powder, black pepper together. Toss potato wedges in oil.
  • Pre-heat oven to 185â€™C.
  • Place wedges on grease proof baking tray. Ensure wedges are evenly spread and a singular layer thick.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place tray in the oven for 15 minutes before removing and turning wedges to ensure an even colour. Place back in the oven for a further 15 minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Potato wedges go great with salsa, relish or dips.

Storing instructions

Consume when made.
