Crepes

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Plain Flour 150g
Table Salt 1 tsp
Whole Milk 315ml
Medium Eggs 2x1
Caster Sugar 1 tsp
Unsalted Butter 40g
Vegetable Oil 80ml

Yield: Serves 8-10

Preparation

  • Sift flour, salt and sugar into a bowl.
  • Melt butter in the microwave.
  • Whisk melted butter, milk and eggs.
  • Make a well in the middle of the flour mix and pour in the liquid ingredients.
  • Mix gradually with a wooden spoon and then use an electric whisk to mix to a smooth consistency.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place 15cm frying pan over a medium/high heat.
  • Add 10ml of vegetable oil to the pan and distribute evenly.
  • Add a ladleâ€™s worth of Pancake batter to the centre of the pan.
  • Spread batter evenly around the pan and fry for 2 minutes before flipping.
  • Only flip pancake once bottom side is golden brown.
  • Cook for 2 minutes on other side before serving.
  • Repeat process until all the batter is used up.

Serving Suggestions

Crepes go well with fresh strawberries, raspberries, and blue berries, fresh cream, maple syrup, golden syrup, bananas, toffee sauce and even bacon

Storing instructions

Allow crepes to come down to room temperature before putting in an air-tight container and placing in a refrigerator. This recipe contains Egg and should be kept for no longer than 4 days.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave and warm for 1 minute.
