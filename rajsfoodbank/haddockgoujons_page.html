Haddock Goujons

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable Oil 2 Litres
Haddock Fillets 400g
Plain Flour 100g
Table Salt 1 tsp
Medium Eggs 2x1
Whole Milk 70ml
Sliced Granary Bread 6x1

Yield: 4 portions

Preparation

  • Cut haddock fillets into 1cm thick strips .
  • Mix flour with salt and pepper.
  • Whisk eggs with milk.
  • Put sliced bread in food processor and blend into crumbs.
  • Pane haddock in flour, egg and bread crumb. Shake any excess flour and egg off to ensure an even coating of bread crumbs.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a sauce pan over a medium/high heat and add all the vegetable oil.
  • Once the oil reaches 170â€™C, lower heat and fry haddock in batches. Fry for 3-4 minutes before removing from the pan.
  • Repeat the process until all the haddock is fried.

Serving Suggestions

Haddock goujons go great with tartar sauce.

Storing instructions

Consume on the day.
