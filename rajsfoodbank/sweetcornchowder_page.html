Sweetcorn Chowder

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Stock 2.4 litres
Frozen Sweetcorn 1.5kg
White Onion 3x1
Maris piper potatoes 600g
Sage Leaves 10x1
Chilli flakes 1 tsp
Vegetable Oil 40ml

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and slice white onion.
  • Peel and dice potato in even pieces.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a large saucepan over a medium/high heat and add the vegetable oil.
  • Add onions to the pan and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Once onions are soft, add chilli flakes and sage leaves and cook for 3 minutes.
  • Add potatoes to the pan and submerge with chicken stock.
  • Bring to the boil and reduce to the simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  • Once potato is cooked, add sweetcorn and cook for a further 4 minutes.
  • Place chowder in a food processor or, alternatively, use a hand blender to blend smooth.
  • Pass soup through a chinois to remove any big bits.
  • Return to pan and season to taste before serving.

Serving Suggestions

This soup goes well with crusty bread and lots of butter.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature before placing in an airtight container and placing in the fridge. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Gently bring back up to temperature in saucepan on stove. Alternatively, warm in microwave for 3 minutes, stirring every 1 minute to ensure even warming.
