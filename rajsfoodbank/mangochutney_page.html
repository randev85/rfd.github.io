Mango Chutney

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Mango 4x1
Garlic Cloves 4x1
Caster Sugar 450g
Baking Apple 2x1
English Mustard Powder 15g
Root Ginger 2 Inches
White Wine Vinegar 600ml
Cayenne Pepper 15g

Yield: 700g portions

Preparation

  • Peel and deseed mangoes before slicing, laying on tray and sprinkling with salt. Leave in fridge overnight and remove any excess liquid from tray.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves
  • Peel and core baking apples.
  • Peel and grate ginger.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place medium sized saucepan over a low heat and add garlic, ginger, apples, mustard, vinegar and cayenne pepper.
  • Once sugar has dissolved, bring to the boil, add sliced mangoes and reduce to the simmer. Cook for 30 minutes until chutney is thicker in consistency.

Serving Suggestions

Mango Chutney can be used as an addition to traditional Indian curries .

Storing instructions

Store in an airtight container and consume within 1 month of making.
