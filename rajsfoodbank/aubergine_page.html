Aubergine

Soil Requirements

Aubergine requires a slightly acidic soil of between 5.5 to 6.0 ph. Test your soil with a soil ph meter and if required, add alkaline by either using lime, mulches or a bicarbonate of soda solution (1 tsp per 2 litres of water). Alternatively, if there is large concentrations of alkaline in the soil, treat with a vinegar solution (1 tablespoon per 2 litres) until ph levels are right.

Aubergine also require semi loose soil that is free from weeds and roots. This is because the plant needs good drainage.

Space Requirements

Area 20cm²
Height 50cm

Additional Information

The aubergine plant likes warm climates and is best grown in a greenhouse. Once the flowers begin to fruit, it is important to support the stems with rigid sticks to support the weight.

Seed Germination & Planting

Germinate aubergine seeds in February. Sow seeds in compost pots and in a propagator at between 18-21â€™C. Ensure compost stays moist during this period. Once seeds have sprouted, transfer from compost pot to another pot that has a 9cm diameter and height. Add 5cm of compost to the soil and water well before placing in a green house. Place somewhere where the plant gets plenty of sunlight.

After about a month of growing, transfer from 9cm pot to a pot with 23cm diameter. Add 5cm of compost to the soil of the new pot and water well. Ensure you use a ridged stick and ties to support the stem of the plant at this stage.

Water Requirements

Water plant every day to ensure the soil remains moist and mist the leaves twice a day to help fruit set.

Once the plant bares fruit, start to feed it with a high potassium liquid fertiliser on a two week basis.

Pruning Requirements

Once the plant has reached a height of 30cm, remove the tip from main stem.

Once 5-6 flowers have started to fruit, remove the remaining flowers.

Yeild:

5-6 decent sized aubergines.

Harvesting Guidelines

Cut each fruit from August onwards when they've grown about 15cm long and the skin surface is still shiny.

Storing Guidlines

Store aubergines at room temperature and away from direct sunlight. Consume within 2 weeks of harvesting.
