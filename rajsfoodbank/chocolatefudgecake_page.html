Chocolate Fudge Cake

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
70% Dark Chocolate 250g
Milk Chocolate 150g
Unsalted Butter 300g
Self-raising Flour 125g
Plain Flour 125g
Cocoa Powder 25g
Muscovado Sugar 200g
Caster Sugar 200g
Medium Eggs 3x1
Créme Fraiche 260g
Icing Sugar 200g
Vanilla Essence 2 tsp
Double Cream 150ml

Yield: 10-12 portions

Preparation

  • Grease and line two 20cm sandwich tins.
  • Break 200g of 70% dark chocolate in a glass bowl. Add 200 of butter and 100ml water to the bowl.
  • Break the remaining 50g of 70% dark chocolate into a bowl and add the remaining 100g of butter.
  • Sift Both flours and cocoa powder in a separate bowl. Add muscovado sugar and caster sugar to flour before mixing.
  • Mix Eggs and créme fraiche together.
  • Pre-heat oven to 170â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place chocolate, butter and water over hot simmering water to melt
  • Once chocolate and butter have melted, mix with egg and créme fraiche until smooth.
  • Fold in flour and divide batter between both cake tins. Place cake tin in the oven for 30-35 minutes.
  • To Create Ganache, heat the double cream in a saucepan over a low heat and add milk chocolate, and 1 tsp of vanilla essence. Whisk until chocolate has melted and remove from heat.
  • Remove cake from oven and allow to cool to room temperature.
  • Spread Ganache over first half of cake before placing the second half on top of it.
  • For the chocolate buttercream icing, place the other bowl of chocolate and butter over hot simmering water and melt. Once melted, sift in icing sugar and add the remaining 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  • Once all the icing sugar has dissolved, remove from the heat and pour butter cream on top of cake. Allow to cool slightly and spread with a pallet knife.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with cream and a cup of tea.

Storing instructions

Cover with clingfilm and place in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
