Sweet & Sour Chicken

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breast 6 fillets
Pineapple 1x1
Vegetable Oil 45ml
White Onion 3x1
Red Pepper 2x1
Green Pepper 2x1
Cornflour 90g
Spring Onion 6 stalks
Pineapple Juice 900ml
Garlic Clovesl 6 cloves
Root Ginger 2 inches
Dark Soy Sauce 45ml
White Wine Vinegar 90ml
Brown Sugar 120g
Tomato Ketchup 100g
Chilli Flakes 8g
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Dice chicken breast into 1 inch cubes
  • Peel, core and dice pineapple into 1 inch cubes.
  • Peel and dice white onion into 1 inch cubes.
  • Deseed and dice peppers into 1 inch.
  • Peel and grate ginger.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Thinly slice spring onions on the diagonal.
  • Using half the cornflour, gradually mix with 15ml of pineapple juice until smooth.
  • Coat the diced chicken with the remaining cornflour and season with salt.
  • Mix remaining pineapple juice with crushed garlic, soy sauce, grated ginger, vinegar, sugar, ketchup and chilli flakes before setting aside.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a large saucepan over a medium/high heat.
  • Add half the vegetable oil to the pan and fry off the onions and peppers for 5 minutes.
  • Remove peppers and onions, add remaining oil and fry diced chicken for 6 minutes.
  • Return peppers and onions back to the pan, along with diced pineapple and pineapple juice mix.
  • Bring to the boil and reduce to the simmer for 8 minutes.
  • Once chicken is cooked, add corn flour mix and stir until sauce thickens.
  • Season to taste, garnish with sliced spring onions and serve.

Serving Suggestions

Sweet & sour chicken is great with sticky rice and egg-fried rice.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in a saucepan and slowly bring it up to temperature whilst stirring. Alternatively, place in the microwave and heat foe 3-4 minutes.
