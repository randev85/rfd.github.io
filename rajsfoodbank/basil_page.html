Basil

Soil Requirements

Basil require an acidic soil of between 5.5 to 6.5 ph. Test your soil with a soil ph meter and if required, add alkaline by either using lime, mulches or a bicarbonate of soda solution (1 tsp per 2 litres of water). Alternatively, if there is large concentrations of alkaline in the soil, treat with a vinegar solution (1 tablespoon per 2 litres) until ph levels are right.

Basil also requires a semi loose soil that is free from weeds and roots. This is because the plant needs good drainage.

Space Requirements

Area 15cm²
Height 15cm

Seed Germination & Planting

Sow basil seed indoors between the end of February to the start of July. Fill 3-inch compost pots with a soil and compost mix before placing the seed in the soil at a 5mm depth. Ensure the soil stays moist and place a clear plastic food bag over it. Secure the bag with a rubber band and add a few small air holes to it. Place pot on a window ceil that receives partial light throughout the day.

Once seed has sprouted and is 3cm in height, remove bag and keep at a temperature of at least 16’C. Ensure you water the plant every other day in the mornings to keep the soil moist.

If you’ve sown your seeds indoors from February, it is imperative that transfer the plant to a bigger pot around the start of April.

The plant can be transferred to the outdoors towards the end of May. Be sure to treat the soil with 5cm or organic material or compost before transferring. Choose an area that is sheltered from wind and that gets lots of sunshine.

Water Requirements

Basil requires watering once a day and prefers it in the morning as opposed to the evenings.

Pruning Requirements

Keep plants bushy and productive by pinching the tips of branches regularly and remove any flowers that start to develop. If the plant is allowed to produce flowers, it loses flavor.

Yeild:

340-360g on average per plant.

Harvesting Guidelines

Start picking leaves in June as required. Pick leaves from the top to encourage a bushier plant.

Storing Guidlines

Pick, wash and consume when needed.
