Black Forrets Gateau

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Plain Flour 265g
Caster Sugar 400g
Cocoa Powder 65g
Baking Powder 1½ tsp
Bicarbonate of Soda ¾ tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Medium Eggs 3x1
Vegetable Oil 120ml
Milk 240ml
Vanilla Essence 15ml
Cherry Jam 1x370g
Home Bake Glace Cherries 1x200g
Double Cream 750ml
Icing Sugar 750ml
70% Dark Chocolate 200g

Yield: 10-12 portions

Preparation

  • Grease and line two 23cm round sandwich tins.
  • Pre-Heat Oven to 170â€™C.
  • Sift plain flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, cocoa powder and salt in a bowl. Add caster and mix before creating a well in the middle of it.
  • In a separate bowl, use an electric whisk to mix egg, milk, vegetable oil and vanilla essence together.
  • Pour the wet ingredients into the centre of the well you created in the dry ingredients. Use an electric whisk to combine all ingredients by starting off on a slow speed and increasing speed once the dry is combined with the wet.
  • Pour cake batter in sandwich tins.
  • Grate Dark Chocolate
  • Place Double cream in a bowl and sift icing sugar into it. Use an electric whisk to beat cream so that it leaves a firm peak. Transfer to a piping bag with a star nozzle attachment before placing in the fridge.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place Sandwich tins in the oven on the middle shelf for 35-40 minutes.
  • Remove sponges from oven and leave to cool to room tempreture.
  • Spread half cherry jam over the top of the first cake and apply a third off the whipped cream to it. Place the second cake on top of that and spread the remaining cherry jam on top of this.
  • To decorate the cake, apply a third of the whipped cream to the side of the cake and spread evenly with a pallet knife. Apply grated chocolate to the cream on the side of the cake.
  • Decorate the top of the cake by piping the remaining whipped cream on the cherry jam. Pipe in concentric circles working your way from outside to in. Finish of the cake by adding glace cherries to the top of it.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with a cup of tea.

Storing instructions

Cover with clingfilm and place in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
