Lemon Drizzle

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted Butter 225g
Caster Sugar 225g
Self-raising Flour 275g
Baking Powder 2 tsp
Medium Egg 4x1
Lemons 2x1
Icing Sugar 175g

Yield: 32 portions

Preparation

  • Cut butter into cubes.
  • Sift flour and baking powder into a bowl.
  • Crack and whisk eggs.
  • Zest and juice lemons.
  • Mix lemon juice and icing sugar to form a paste.
  • Line a deep baking tray, 20x30cm, with greaseproof paper.
  • Pre-heat oven to 180â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place Unsalted butter in microwave for 15-20 seconds until soft.
  • Use an electric hand whisk to mix the caster sugar and unsalted butter together. Whisk until mixture is pale and fluffy.
  • Add whisked egg gradually in 4 stages whilst mixing with electric hand whisk.
  • Add lemon zest and sifted flour to the mixture. Mix on a slow speed to begin with and once flour is combined, on a high speed for 2-3 minutes.
  • Transfer cake batter to deep baking tray and evenly smooth over with a spatula.
  • Place tray in the oven for 35 minutes.
  • Remove from oven, pierce with knife at 1 inch intervals and spread on icing sugar paste. Allow paste to dissolve and seep into cake
  • Allow cake to cool before slicing into 32 portions.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with a cup of tea

Storing instructions

Store in refrigerator. Consume with 1 week of making.
