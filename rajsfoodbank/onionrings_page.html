Onion Rings

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Large White Onion 1x1
Self-raising Flour 250g
Sparkling Water 190ml
Salt 1 tsp
Vegetable Oil 2 Litres

Yield: 2-3 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and slice white onion into 1cm thick rings.
  • Toss onion rings in 100g of self-raising flour.
  • Whisk sparkling water with remaining flour before seasoning with salt and pepper.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place medium sized sauce pan over a medium/high heat. Add vegetable oil to the pan and bring to 180â€™C in temperature.
  • Coat the onions ring in batter, one at a time and slowly lower into hot oil. Fry 4-5 onions rings at a time and turn after 2 minutes to ensure an even golden-brown colour. Cook 3-4 minutes and repeat the process until all ring are ready.

Serving Suggestions

Serve onion rings with a burger or a selection of dips.

Storing instructions

consume when made.
