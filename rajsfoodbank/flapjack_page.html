Flapjack

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Rolled Oats 375g
Unsalted Butter 190g
Light Brown Sugar 190g
Golden Syrup 67.5g

Yield: 32 pieces

Preparation

  • Pre-heat oven to 165â€™C.
  • Line baking tray with greaseproof paper on base and sides.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place butter, syrup and sugar in medium sized saucepan over medium heat and dissolve sugar to create smooth liquid.
  • Add rolled oats to liquid coat thoroughly.
  • Evenly distribute oats in baking tray and smooth over with the back of a wet tablespoon.
  • Place flapjack in oven for 15 minutes until surface is golden brown..
  • Allow to cool before portioning out into 32 pieces.

Storing instructions

Store in refrigerator. Consume with 1 week of making.
