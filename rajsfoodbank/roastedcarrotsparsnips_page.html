Roasted Carrots & Parsnips

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Carrot 200g
Parsnips 200g
Vegetable Oil 60ml
Honey 60g
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 4-6 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and cut carrot in half length ways.
  • Peel and cut parsnip in half length ways.
  • Toss carrots and parsnips in vegetable oil before seasoning with salt and pepper. Place carrots and parsnips on baking tray lined with grease proof paper.
  • Pre-heat oven to 190â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place tray in oven for 10-12 minutes until carrot and parsnips are semi-cooked.
  • Remove tray from oven a drizzle honey over vegetables. Toss vegetables to ensure an even coating before placing in the oven for a further 10-12 minutes.

Serving Suggestions

serve alongside a roast dinner.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and store in an airtight contain before placing in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.

Reheating Instructions

Place in microwave for 2-3 minutes.
