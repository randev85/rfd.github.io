BBQ Pulled Chicken

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breast 6 fillets
White Onion 2x1
Vegetable Oil 45ml
Garlic Cloves 3 Cloves
Smoked Paprika 2 tsp
Table Salt 2 tsp
BBQ sauce 600ml

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and slice onions.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Pre-heat oven to 160â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place cast iron pan on a medium/high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Fry off the onions for 5 minutes.
  • Add smoked paprika, salt and garlic and cook for for a further 2 minutes.
  • Add BBQ sauces and chicken, cover pan with lid and place in the oven for 1-1½ hours.
  • Remove from the oven and pull chicken apart into strands before mixing with sauce and seasoning to taste.

Serving Suggestions

BBQ chicken is great served in a bap or with nachos.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in a saucepan and slowly bring it up to temperature whilst stirring. Alternatively, place in the microwave and heat foe 3-4 minutes.
