Poached Pear

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Caster Sugar 300g
Cinnamon Stick 1 Stick
Vanilla Essence 1 tsp
Lemon 1x1
Ripe Pears 8x1

Yield: 8 portions

Preparation

  • Peel Pears.
  • Juice Lemon.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place pears in a sauce pan and add 1 litre of water, cinnamon stick, vanilla essence, lemon juice and caster sugar.
  • Place saucepan over a medium/high heat and bring to the boil before reducing to the simmer. Cook for 8-10 minutes until knife goes through pear very easily.
  • Remove pear from liquor and allow to cool. Leave sauce pan on the stove to reduce liquor by half, so that it turns into a syrup. Discard cinnamon stick and pour syrup over pear before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with vanilla ice cream.

Storing instructions

Cover with clingfilm and place in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
