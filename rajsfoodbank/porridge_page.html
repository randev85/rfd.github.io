Porridge

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Rolled Oats 200g
Whole Milk 1300ml
Unsalted Butter 100g
Caster Sugar 3 tsp
Table Salt 1 tsp

Yield: Serves 5-6

Cooking Instructions

  • Place sauce pan of milk and butter over a medium heat.
  • Once milk is warm, add rolled oats and stir occasionally.
  • Allow porridge to thicken before add salt and caster sugar.
  • Adjust viscosity with hot water for desired consistancy.

Serving Suggestions

Porridge is a very versatile breakfast item. I like to add seeds & nuts for texture, dried & fresh fruit for flavour and honey or syrup for sweetness.

Storing instructions

Porridge should be made to order and not reheated from cold. This ensures that best quality is achieved.
