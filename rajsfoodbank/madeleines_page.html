Madelines

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Medium Eggs 2x1
Vanilla Essence ½ tsp
Lemon 1x1
Icing Sugar 125g
Plain Flour 100g
Baking Powder ¼ tsp
Unsalted Butter 125g

Yield: 24 portions

Preparation

  • Zest lemon.
  • Using an electric hand whisk, whisk eggs with lemon zest and vanilla on a high speed for 4-5 minutes.
  • Gradually sift icing sugar with egg on a slow speed.Once icing sugar is incorporated, whisk of high speed for a further 5-6 minutes until mixture is thicker and has a gloss.
  • Sift flour and baking powder in separate bowl.
  • Completely melt butter in microwave for 1 minute.
  • Incorporate sifted flour into egg mix by gently folding in with a rubber spatula. Do this ¼ sifted flour at a time.
  • Gently fold in melted butter.
  • Apply butter to a madeleine baking tray and dust mould with even and light layer of flour.
  • Pre-heat oven to 190â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Spoon madeleine batter into moulds and place in oven for 10-12 minutes until edges are golden brown and middle springs back after touch.
  • Cool on wire rack.

Storing instructions

Store in air-tight container. Consume with 1 week of making.
