Kung Pao Chicken

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Dark Soy Sauce 25ml
Chinese Rice Wine Vinegar 30ml
Corn flour 20g
Chicken Breast 4 Fillets
Balsamic Vinegar 15ml
Hoisin Sauce 1 tsp
Sesame Oil 1 tsp
Caster Sugar 10g
Ground Szechwan Pepper ½ tsp
Vegetable Oil 30ml
Dried Whole Red Chillies 8x1
White Onion 2x1
Garlic Cloves 2 Cloves
Root Ginger 1 Inch
Dry Roasted Peanuts 60g

Yield: 4 portions

Preparation

  • Cut chicken breast into 1 inch cubes and marinade in 20ml of soy sauce, 15g of corn flour and the Chinese rice wine vinegar for 10 minutes.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Peel and grate root ginger.
  • Peel and slice white onions.
  • Mix balsamic vinegar, remaining soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, sugar, remaining corn flour and Szechwan pepper in a bowl before setting aside.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a medium sized wok over a medium/high heat and vegetable oil.
  • Once oil starts to smoke, add whole chillies and fry for 30 seconds.
  • Then add chicken and stir-fry for 5-6 minutes until chicken starts to brown.
  • Add sliced white onions, garlic and ginger before stir-frying for a further 1-2 minutes.
  • Add the balsamic and soy sauce mix to the pan and stir-fry for 3 more minutes before serving and garnishing with roasted peanuts.

Serving Suggestions

Kung Pao Chicken goes well with sticky rice or egg noodles.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave and heat foe 2-3 minutes.
