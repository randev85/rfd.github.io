Pizza Bianca

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Strong White Flour 1kg
Dried Yeast 10g
Olive Oil 40ml
Table Salt 3 tsp

Yield: 5 Bases

Preparation

  • Sift Flour and salt and mix in yeast.
  • Make well in the centre of the flour and add 160ml of lukewarm water.
  • Mix flour into water until dough comes away from the side of the bowl.
  • Flour work surface and tip out dough before folding several times.
  • Wait 10 minutes and repeat folding before placing in a bowl, cling filming and leaving in the fridge for 12-24 hours.
  • Remove from the fridge and fold dough 2 more times.
  • Divide dough into 5 equal pieces and roll out
  • Place Pizza Stone in oven and pre-heat to 185’C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place pizza base in the oven for 10-12 minutes.

Serving Suggestions

Bread can used for making pizza or garlic bread

Storing instructions

Once cool, store in a refrigerator. Pizza bread should be consumed within 4 days.

Reheating Instructions

Place in pre-heated oven 160’C, for 5-8 minutes.

suitable for toasting.
