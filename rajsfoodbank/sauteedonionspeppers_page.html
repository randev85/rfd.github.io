Sautéed Peppers & Onions

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Red Onion 1x1
Red Bell Pepper 3x1
Vegetable Oil 20ml
Smoked Paprika 1 tsp
Salt & Pepper To Season

Yield: 3-4 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and slice red onion.
  • Deseed and slice red bell peppers.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a frying pan over a high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Add sliced red onion, bell peppers and smoked paprika before sautéing until vegetables are soft and have colour. Season with salt and pepper.

Serving Suggestions

serve as a filling to burritos or fajitas.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and store in an airtight contain before placing in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.

Reheating Instructions

Place in microwave for 30 seconds.
