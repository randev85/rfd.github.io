Genoise Sponge

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Medium Eggs 4x1
Caster Sugar 125g
Unsalted Butter 100g
Plain Flour 100g

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Grease and line 20cm round sandwich tin.
  • Pre-Heat Oven to 160â€™C.
  • Sift plain flour.
  • Melt butter in microwave. Separate the clarified butter from the milk solids and discard the milk solids.
  • Mix egg and caster sugar in glass bowl.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place bowl of egg and sugar over hot simmer water and use an electric whisk to mix it. Whisk for 3-5 minutes until mixture becomes light and has doubled in size.
  • Remove bowl from heat and whisk for a further 2-3 minutes until you can draw a ribbon with mixture and it holds.
  • Carefully pour melted butter around the outside of the bowl and gently fold in with a figure of eight motion using a rubber spatula.
  • Add sifted flour half at a time and fold in gently using a figure of eight motion. Do not over mix as this will mean the mixture loses it air bubbles.
  • Once all the flour is completely mixed, gently pour batter into cake tin and bake for 30-35 minutes. Check sponge after 30 minutes and not before, as this might make you sponge collapse.
  • Remove sponges from oven and leave to cool to room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

Dress with whipped cream and a compote.

Storing instructions

Cover with clingfilm and place in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
