Celeriac Soup

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted Butter 60g
Celeriac 1x1
Potato 3x1
Leek 1x1
White Onion 1x1
Garlic Cloves 2x1
Chicken Stock 1.4 Litres
Salt & Pepper To Taste

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Peel and dice celeriac.
  • Peel and dice potatoes.
  • wash and slice leek.
  • Peel and slice white onion.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a large saucepan over a medium/high heat and melt unsalted butter.
  • Add the sliced onion, leek, potato and celeriac to pan and sweat on a low heat for 10 minutes.
  • Add crushed garlic and cook for a further 2 minutes.
  • Add chicken stock before bringing to the boil and reducing to the simmer.
  • After 20-25 minutes, check to see if celeriac is soft and tender.
  • Transfer ingredients to a food processor, or alternatively, blend with a hand blender.
  • Pass soup through a chinois to ensure smooth consistency before returning to sauce pan.
  • Season soup to taste.

Serving Suggestions

The soup goes well with crusty bread and lots of butter or with crotons.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature before placing in an airtight container and placing in the fridge. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Gently bring back up to temperature in saucepan on stove. Alternatively, warm in microwave for 3 minutes, stirring every 1 minute to ensure even warming.
