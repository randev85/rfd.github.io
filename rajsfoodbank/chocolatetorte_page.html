Baked Chocolate Torte

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted Butter 200g
70% Dark Chocolate 200g
Large Eggs 4x1
Caster Sugar 200g
Plain Flour 50g
Ground Almonds 50g
Table Salt ¼ tsp
Cocoa Powder For Dusting

Yield: 8-10 portions

Preparation

  • Break dark chocolate and place in a bowl.
  • Cube butter and add it to bowl of chocolate.
  • Whisk egg and sugar with electric whisk for 5-6 minutes until mixture is thick and pale.
  • Sift flour and ground almond.
  • Butter 23cm spring form cake tin.
  • Pre-heat oven to 160â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place bowl of chocolate and butter over simmer water and melt.
  • Once melted, fold in to egg and sugar mixture gently with a metal spoon. Add flour and ground almonds and fold in too.
  • Carefully pour batter into cake tin and place tin in the oven for 35-40 minutes.
  • Once torte is cooked, dust with cocoa powder and cool to room tempreture.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with cream or ice cream.

Storing instructions

Cover with clingfilm and place in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.
