Crispy Pork Belly

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Pork Belly 1.5kg
Maldon Sea Salt 2 tsp
Ground Black Pepper 1 tsp
Chilli Powder 1 tsp
Smoked Paprika 1 tsp
Ground Coriander 40ml
Vegetable Oil 400ml

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Mix paprika, salt, pepper, coriander and vegetable oil.
  • Rub skin and flesh of belly with paprika oil and place on an elevated wire rack before placing the wire rack in a roasting tin.
  • Add 500ml of water to roasting tin and cover with 2 layers of foil, ensuring a tight seal.
  • Pre-heat oven to 150’C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place roasting tin in oven for 3 hours.
  • Remove foil from tin.
  • Turn up heat of oven to 200’C and belly for 20-30 minutes before serving.
  • Allow to rest for 10 minutes before cutting with sharp knife into squares.

Serving Suggestions

Serve crispy pork belly with green beans and creamy mash.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature, seal in an airtight container and place in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making.

Reheating Instructions

Heat in microwave for 2-3 minutes.
