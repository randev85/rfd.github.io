Sweet Chilli Sauce

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Rice Wine Vinegar 120ml
Caster Sugar 180g
Garlic Cloves 4 Cloves
Dark Soy Sauce 15ml
Cayenne Pepper 2 Cloves
Dried Chilli Flakes 60g
Cornflour 15g

Yield: 400g

Preparation

  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Mix cornflour with 20ml of cold water.

Cooking Instructions

  • Put rice wine vinegar, 250ml of water, sugar, crushed garlic, soy sauce and cayenne pepper in a wok. Place over a high heat.
  • Bring to the boil and reduce to the simmer for 45 minutes.
  • Add chilli flakes and cook for a further 2 minutes.
  • Add cornflour mixture to pan and stir until thick.

Serving Suggestions

Sweet chilli sauce is good with prawn crackers.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature. Consume within 1 month of making. Store in the fridge
