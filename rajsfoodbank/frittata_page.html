Frittata

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Medium Egg 10x1
Double Cream 300ml
Salt & Pepper To Season
Tomatoes 6x1
Spring Onions 6 stalks

Yield: 4-5 portions

Preparation

  • Crack eggs in a bowl and whisk. Add double cream and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  • Thinly slice spring onion on the diagonal.
  • Cut tomatoes into eight equal pieces, place on baking tray and drizzle with oil, salt and pepper.
  • Line a deep baking dish with greaseproof paper.
  • Pre-heat oven to 180â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place tray of tomatoes in the oven for 20 minutes.
  • Once tomatoes are cooked, add with spring onion to egg mix and pour into deep baking dish.
  • Place baking dish in the oven and cook frittata for 20-25 minutes.

Serving Suggestions

Frittata is a light and summery dish and goes nicely with a balanced salad.

Storing instructions

Allow Frittata to cool completely before storing in an air tight container. It should be consumed within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Frittata can be reheated in a microwave. Once reheated, it is good practice not to cool down and heat again.
