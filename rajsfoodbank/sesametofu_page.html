Sesame Fried Tofu

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Firm Tofu 1x320g
Vegetable Oil 300ml
Garlic Powder 1tsp
Spring Onion 1 Stalk
White Sesame Seeds 30g
Black Sesame Seeds 30g
Cornflour 60g
Table Salt 1½ tsp
Chinese Five Spice ½ tsp

Yield: 2-3 portions

Preparation

  • Cut firm tofu into 1 inch cubes.
  • Finely slice spring onion.
  • Mix cornflour, sesame seeds, garlic powder, Chinese five spice and salt together.
  • Coat tofu pieces in an even layer of cornflour mix.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a frying pan over a medium/high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Fry tofu pieces for 1-2 minutes each side until golden brown.
  • Garnish with sliced spring onions.

Serving Suggestions

This dish goes great with a soy dipping sauce.

Storing instructions

Place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave for 2-3 minutes.
