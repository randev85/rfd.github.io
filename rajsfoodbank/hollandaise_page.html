Hollandaise Sauce

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted Butter 250g
Egg Yolks 4x1
White Wine Vinegar 1 tsp
Table Salt Pinch
Cayenne Pepper Pinch

Yield: 350g

Preparation

  • Crack egg and seperate yolk from white.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place butter in small pan and on a gentle low heat. Once melted, allow to split and remove the solids from the surface.
  • In a separate saucepan, add water and bring to the boil before reducing to the simmer.
  • Add egg yolks, white wine vinegar, salt and a slash of cold water to a bowl and whisk.
  • Place bowl over simmering water and whisk yolks for 1 minute until colour is pale.
  • Remove bowl from the heat and whisk in clarified butter in gradually until a creamy consistency is achieved.

Serving Suggestions

Hollandaise sauce works well with eggs benedict.

Storing instructions

Consume when made
