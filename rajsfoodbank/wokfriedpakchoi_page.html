Wok-Fried Pak Choi

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Pak Choi 3 heads
Vegetable Oil 20ml
Red Chilli 1x1 or ½x1
Root Ginger 1 inch
Garlic Clove 1 Clove
Dark Soy Sauce 20ml

Yield: 3-4 portions

Preparation

  • Wash and separate Pak Choi leaves.
  • Peel and crush garlic clove.
  • Peel and grate root ginger.
  • Finley slice red chilli.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a wok over a high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Evenly coat wok with oil and once the oil starts to smoke, add garlic, ginger and chilli before stir-frying until garlic has browned.
  • Add Pak Choi to the pan and cook until leaves have started to wilt.
  • Add dark soy sauce before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve alongside sticky rice and sesame tofu.

Storing instructions

Cool to room temperature and store in an airtight contain before placing in the fridge. Consume with 4 days of making.

Reheating Instructions

Place in microwave for 1 minute.
