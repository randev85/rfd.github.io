Ribeye Steak

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Ribeye Steak 1x400g
Vegetable Oil 30ml
Rosemary 2 sprigs
Thyme 2 sprigs
Garlic Cloves 2 Cloves
Unsalted Butter 80g
Maldon Sea Salt 1½tsp
Cracked Black Pepper 1½tsp

Yield: 1 portion

Preparation

  • Remove ribeye from fridge 30 minutes before cooking to acclimatise to room temperature.
  • Season ribeye steak with salt and pepper.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a large pan over a medium/high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Fry Ribeye Steak for 2 minutes on each side. Add butter, garlic, rosemary and thyme and cook for 4 minutes each side whilst spooning over butter. This should cook a 1 inch thick steak to a medium rare.
  • Leave to rest for 5 minutes under foil before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with chips and béarnaise sauce.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature. Consume within 4 days of making. Store in the fridge

Reheating Instructions

Place in the microwave for 1-2 minutes.
