Chocolate Orange Cake

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted Butter 100g
Cocoa Powder 50g
Medium Eggs 4x1
Whole Milk 60ml
Self-raining Flour 175g
Baking Powder 1½ tsp
Caster Sugar 300g
Orange 1x1
70% Dark Chocolate 150g
Double Cream 150g
Salt ½ tsp

Yield: 8-10 portions

Preparation

  • Place butter in the microwave for 20-25 seconds until soft.
  • Grease 2x20cm round sandwich tins and line bases with greaseproof paper.
  • Mix cocoa powder with 90ml of hot water.
  • Sift flour and baking powder in a bowl.
  • Zest and juice orange.
  • Break chocolate into smaller pieces.
  • Use an electric hand whisk and mix unsalted butter with caster sugar before creaming to a pale complexion.
  • Whisk eggs with butter and sugar mixture, one egg at a time.
  • Add cocoa powder liquid and orange zest before whisking.
  • Add sifted flour and baking powder, mix on a slow setting and once all flour is incorporated, turn the speed up and add milk.
  • Split cake batter over both sandwich tins and smooth out.
  • Pre-heat oven to 160â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place sandwich tin in oven and cook for 25 minutes.
  • Sponge should double in size and test, by placing a knife in the centre and if it comes out clear, its cooked. Place sponges on wire rack to cool.
  • Add double cream to a medium sized saucepan and gently heat. Add orange juice and chocolate pieces before whisking together to create a smooth ganache
  • Split ganache in two portions and spread the first portion on top of one sandwich. Top with the second sandwich and add the remaining ganache over that with a palette knife.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with cream or ice cream.

Storing instructions

Place in an airtight container before placing in the refrigerator. Consume within 4 days of making it.
