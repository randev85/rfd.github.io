Fried Egg

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Medium Egg 1x1
Vegetable Oil 25ml

Yield: 1 Egg

Cooking Instructions

  • Pour vegetable oil in frying pan and place on a low heat for 1 minute.
  • Crack egg and gently pour into oil making sure that the yolk doesnâ€™t break.
  • Cook for 3-5 minutes until egg white has coagulated.
  • The crispier you want your fried egg to be, the higher the heat you will need. The higher heat, the shorter the cooking time required.

Serving Suggestions

Fried egg goes great on top of toast or in a bap.

Storing instructions

Consume when made.
