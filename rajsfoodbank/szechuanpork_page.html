Szechuan Pork

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable Oil 50ml
Pork Fillet 1.2kg
Garlic Cloves 3 Cloves
Tender Stem Broccoli 700g
Red Onion 4x1
Dark Soy Sauce 50ml
Red Wine Vinegar 50ml
Rice Wine Vinegar 50ml
Chilli Powder 2 tsp
Chicken Stock 200ml
Caster Sugar 20g
Cornflour 15g
Salt & Pepper To taste

Yield: 6-8 portions

Preparation

  • Cut pork fillet into 1cm slice before seasoning with salt and pepper.
  • Peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Cut broccoli stem into 3cm pieces.
  • Peel and slice red onions.
  • Mix soy sauce with red wine vinegar, rice wine vinegar, chilli powder, chicken stock, caster sugar and cornflour in a bowl and set aside.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a large wok over a medium/high heat and add vegetable oil.
  • Add small amount of pork to the wok and fry until golden brown before keeping to one side. Repeat process until all pork is browned.
  • Add 10ml of oil to the wok and fry garlic until golden brown.
  • Add broccoli and red onion and fry for 4-5 minutes.
  • Return pork back to wok and add soy sauce mix before cooking for a further 5 minutes.
  • Season to taste before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with sticky rice or egg noodles.

Storing instructions

Allow to cool to room temperature before placing in an airtight container and placing in the fridge. Consume within 4 days of making it.

Reheating Instructions

Gently bring back up to temperature in saucepan on stove. Alternatively, warm in microwave for 3 minutes, stirring every 1 minute to ensure even warming.
