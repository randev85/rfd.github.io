Salmon En Croute

Recipe Specification

Ingredients List

Ingredients Quantity
Salmon Fillet 1kg
Puff Pastry 1x320g
Unsalted Butter 150g
Dill 80g
Lemon 1x1
Baby Spinach 100g
Medium Egg 1x1

Yield: 4-5 portions

Preparation

  • Deskin and debone salmon fillet.
  • Wash and spin dry spinach leaves.
  • Finely chop dill leaves.
  • Place butter in microwave for 30-40 seconds and mix with dill before seasoning with salt and pepper.
  • Line baking tray with greaseproof paper.
  • Cut puff pastry in half and place one half on baking tray.
  • Crack and whisk egg.
  • Pre-heat oven to 200â€™C.

Cooking Instructions

  • Place a pan over a medium/high heat. Add vegetable oil and sauté spinach leaves.
  • Squeeze liquid out of spinach.
  • Spread spinach on top of first puff pastry half and leave a 1 inch perimeter of puff pastry free from spinach. Place salmon on spinach and spread herb butter on top. Egg wash the 1 inch perimeter and place the second half of puff pastry over everything to enclose. Crimp edges with fork, cut slits in the top of pastry and egg wash surface.
  • Place in the oven for 15-18 minutes.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with fondant potatoes and spring greens.

Storing instructions

Put in an air-tight container and in the refrigerator. Consume within 1 week.

Reheating Instructions

Place in microwave for 2-3 minutes.
